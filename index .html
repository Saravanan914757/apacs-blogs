APACS

About the Brand

APACS, a Malaysian brand, is known for offering high-quality yet affordable badminton rackets, gaining international recognition for their excellent value for money.

Racket Specifications

  • Power-Oriented Rackets: Best for smashes - Example: Z-Ziggler, Lethal 66.
  • Speed-Oriented Rackets: Quick maneuverability - Example: Feather Weight 55, Tantrum 200.
  • All-Round Rackets: Balanced attack & defense - Example: Finapi 232, Blend Duo 88.
